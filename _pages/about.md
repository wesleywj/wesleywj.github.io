## Background

Hi, I am Wesley. I'm a graduate student at the University of Oxford pursuing an MPhil in Sociology & Demography. My interests lie primarily in the intersection between gene and environment (GxE), and how it contributes to inequalities across the life course. I am also interested in exploring differences in life course trajectories through the use of agent-based models as experimental simulations.

You can find my CV [here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1hSUJePiOMuiYPzmdF4ZKEPCzo7fd8Cl5/view?usp=share_link){:target="_blank"}.


## Ongoing projects

Together with Assoc Prof. Nathalie Williams, we utilise an agent-based model to examine how armed conflict in Chitwan Valley, Nepal, produces varying family formation behaviours and demographic outcomes. 

Simultaneously, I am also working with Dr Evelina Akimova to conduct a meta-analysis of GxE and twin studies.
